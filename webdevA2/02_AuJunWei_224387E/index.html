Japanese Manga



Bleach

Summary

Bleach is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo.
Bleach follows the story of Ichigo Kurosaki,
a high school student who can see spirits.
One day, Ichigo meets Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper
who is tasked with hunting down and purifying Hollows,
evil spirits that can harm humans. Ichigo agrees to help Rukia and becomes a Soul Reaper himself.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Shounen

Boruto: Naruto Next Generations

Summary

Boruto: Naruto Next Generations is a Japanese shōnen manga series written
by Ukyō Kodachi and Masashi Kishimoto,
and illustrated by Mikie Ikemoto.
It is the sequel to Kishimoto's Naruto,
following the adventures of Boruto Uzumaki,
the son of Naruto Uzumaki and Hinata Uzumaki.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Martial Arts, Shounen

Hell's Paradise: Jigokuraku

Summary

Hell's Paradise: Jigokuraku is a Japanese shōnen manga series
written by Yuji Kaku. It is set in a world where criminals are
sent to an island called Jigokuraku to mine a rare metal called "benihime."
The manga follows Gabimaru, a death row inmate who is offered a
chance to be pardoned if he can survive Jigokuraku and bring back a sample of benihime.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Horror, Martial Arts, Shounen

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Summary

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese shōnen manga series
written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotōge.
It follows the story of Tanjirō Kamado, a young boy who becomes
a demon slayer after his family is murdered and his younger sister
Nezuko is turned into a demon.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Drama, Fantasy, Horror, Shounen

One Piece

Summary

One Piece is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda.
It follows the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man who becomes a pirate in search of the One Piece,
a treasure left behind by the legendary pirate Gol D. Roger.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Martial Arts, Shounen

My Hero Academia

Summary

My Hero Academia is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi.
It follows the story of Izuku Midoriya,
a boy born without superpowers in a world where they are the norm,
who dreams of becoming a superhero.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, Martial Arts, Shounen

Reincarnated as Slime

Summary

Reincarnated as Slime is a Japanese light novel series written by Fuse and illustrated by Kurone Mishima. It follows the story of a young boy named Sakura who dies in a traffic accident and is reincarnated as a slime creature in a fantasy world.

Genre

Fantasy, Isekai, Comedy, Romance

Vinland Saga

Summary

Vinland Saga is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Makoto Yukimura. It follows the story of Thorfinn Karlsefni, a Viking warrior who becomes a mercenary and sets out to explore the New World. Along the way, he encounters various challenges and allies, and struggles to find his place in a new world.

Genre

Action, Adventure, Historical, Drama, Manga
